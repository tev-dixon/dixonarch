About Us

Dixon Architecture is an architectural design firm based in Ada, Michigan, with a wide range of project experience.

Our work includes urban redevelopment, mixed-use commercial spaces, retail and office buildings, family dentistry offices, branch banks, and pre-engineered manufacturing and warehouse facilities. Each project is approached with the same commitment to context, craft, and the people who will use the space.

Our Values

At our firm, we focus on building strong relationships with our clients, taking the time to fully understand the unique challenges and opportunities each project presents. We believe that our commitment to collaboration—connecting the client, designer, and builder—creates a solid foundation for success.

We approach each project with the goal of capturing the essence of the surrounding community, allowing that inspiration to guide our design process. This results in architecture that is both beautiful and responsible. By honoring the context and history of a place, we can deliver long-term value for our clients and their communities through thoughtful design.
